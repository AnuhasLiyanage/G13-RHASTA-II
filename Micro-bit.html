BBC Micro-Bit



The Micro Bit is an open source hardware ARM-based embedded system designed by the BBC for use in computer education in the United Kingdom.


It was first announced on the launch of BBC's Make It Digital campaign on 12 March 2015


  • 6 July 2015 - The final device design and features

  • September 2015 - to schools

  • October 2015 - to general public



It Has

  1. an ARM Cortex-M0 processor

  2. accelerometer and magnetometer sensors

  3. Bluetooth and USB connectivity

  4. a display consisting of 25 LEDs

  5. two programmable buttons

  6. and can be powered by either USB or an external battery pack.
